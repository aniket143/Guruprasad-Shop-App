What is a shopnearn CRP (Customer Retention Programme) ?
Shopnearn CRP is the loyalty base purchasing system offered by Guruprasad Multi-trade marketing private ltd (GPMMPL)

How do I earn a Loyalty Cash-back?
Shopnearn customer complete a purchase, customer is qualified for loyalty cash back . This loyalty cash back is remitted to your personal bank account by company .loyalty cash back is derived by the company on the basis of overall business done by the company and your regular consistent purchase .Customer register with GPMMPL through shopnearn.co.in , only those customer can avail this loyalty cash back.

How much loyalty Cash back I have earn ?
Every register customer is given a GPMMPL client account . Customer can login into GPMMPL a/c can see his all details in my Income details link how much loyalty cash back they have earn .

What is GPMMPL client a/c ?
Every customer register with GPMMPL is given a special client account . where he /she can see their all transaction , every purchase details , done at any GPMMPL related shop . Here client can see , add their profile details , purchase details , loyalty cash back details , and all other facility available to client.

How do I Pay?
Customer can pay for their purchase through cash on delivery or Online Payment. GPMMPL have various online payment options that includes net banking, credit/debit card (Visa, Master and Maestro) cards. Our trusted payment gateway, with secure encryption ensures that your transaction is safe. Alternatively, one can pay at the time of delivery by cash on Delivery Option. At the time of checkout customer should mention the preferred mode of payment (online payment, cash)

Can I redeem Loyalty Cash-back ?
Currently this facility is not activate by GPMMPL.

Delivery Charge?
There is no delivery charge Above 500 Rupees online purchase.
